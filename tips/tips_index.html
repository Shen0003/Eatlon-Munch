Culinary Tips & Tricks

Enhance your cooking skills with our expert advice

Categories

Cooking Techniques

Master the Art of Searing

Learn how to achieve the perfect sear on meats for enhanced flavour and texture. Start with a hot pan and don't overcrowd it.

Improve Your Knife Skills

Discover techniques to chop, dice, and julienne like a professional chef. Keep your knives sharp and practice proper grip.

Perfect Your Braising Technique

Learn how to braise meats and vegetables for tender, flavourful results. Low and slow is the key to perfect braising.

Malaysian Cooking

Essential Malaysian Spices

Discover the key spices used in Malaysian cuisine and how to use them effectively. Don't forget lemongrass and pandan leaves!

Mastering Wok Cooking

Learn the techniques for perfect stir-frying in a wok, essential for many Malaysian dishes. High heat and quick movements are crucial.

Cooking with Coconut Milk

Explore the various uses of coconut milk in Malaysian cuisine and how to incorporate it into your dishes for rich, creamy flavours.

Flavour Enhancing

Balancing Flavours

Learn how to balance sweet, sour, salty, and bitter flavours in your dishes for a more harmonious taste.

Using Fresh Herbs

Discover how to use fresh herbs to enhance the flavour of your dishes. Add them at the right time to preserve their flavour.

Experiment with Umami

Understand the fifth taste, umami, and how to incorporate it into your cooking through ingredients like mushrooms, soy sauce, and tomatoes.
